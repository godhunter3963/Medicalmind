# MedicalMind Vision

MedicalMind will become an AI-powered nursing platform.

Target Users:
- Nursing Students
- Staff Nurses
- ICU Nurses

Core Features:
1. Nursing AI Chat
2. Drug Information
3. Procedure Library
4. Disease Database
5. Drug Dose Calculator
6. Clinical Case Simulator
7. NCLEX and Exam Preparation

Long-Term Goal:
To become the most trusted AI assistant for nursing education and clinical learning.
